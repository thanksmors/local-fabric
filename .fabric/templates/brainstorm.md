## Capture Rule

- If a durable choice is made, create/update a decision record in `knowledge/decisions/`.
- If execution requires handoff or multiple steps, create/update a plan in `knowledge/plans/`.
- If external research informed the options, create/update a research note in `knowledge/research/`.
- Do not create a permanent brainstorm transcript unless explicitly requested.
